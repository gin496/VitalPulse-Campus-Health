### **Project Description**

**VitaPulse** is an integrated health-management ecosystem specifically engineered to bridge the gap between students and university medical services. By applying **Agile methodologies**, we have moved away from traditional, rigid booking systems toward a flexible, responsive platform that evolves based on student feedback.

The system provides three core value propositions:

* 
**Seamless Appointment Scheduling:** Eliminates manual queues by allowing students to book, reschedule, or cancel clinic visits in real-time.


* 
**Digital Health Dashboard:** Offers a secure, centralized interface for students to monitor their medical history and upcoming appointments.


* 
**Proactive Well-Being Support:** Features an automated notification system that ensures students never miss a consultation, directly supporting **SDG 3's** mission of timely medical support.



Developed through a series of **Sprints**, MediSwift utilizes a **Throwaway Prototyping** model to validate UI/UX decisions before final implementation, ensuring the end product is both intuitive and highly efficient.
